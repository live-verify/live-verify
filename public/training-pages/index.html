Certificates & Receipts for Testing

📱 About These Training Pages

These training pages demonstrate Live Verify using fictional documents including graduation certificates from Terry Pratchett's Unseen University and receipts from various retailers.

Each document can be scanned using the Live Verify webapp to verify its authenticity.
